Chi-Square Test

Abstract

In the dataset under consideration, numerical columns encompass a range of health-related metrics, including age, mortality status, hospitalization and death indicators, length of hospital stay, survival time, number of co-morbidities, and various physiological parameters such as mean blood pressure, heart rate, and respiratory rate, among others. Additionally, categorical columns capture demographic and contextual information, featuring variables like sex, disease group, education level, and income. To facilitate statistical analysis, particularly the application of the Chi-square test, we aim to focus on specific pairs within the categorical columns.

This targeted approach will provide valuable insights into the relationships and associations present in the dataset, allowing for a more nuanced understanding of the interplay between different variables and their impact on health outcomes. This methodical examination aligns with best practices in statistical analysis, ensuring a comprehensive exploration of the dataset's key dimensions.
